Will quality reduce?

How to compress an image

  1. Select a JPG, PNG, or WebP image.
  2. Choose a quality level and max width.
  3. Download the compressed JPG and check the final size.

For online forms, try a lower quality value first. If the image looks too soft, increase quality and download again.
