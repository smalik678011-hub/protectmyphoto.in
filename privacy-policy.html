Privacy

Privacy Policy

ProtectMyPhoto is designed as a privacy-first browser toolkit. Selected images are processed locally in your browser and are not uploaded to our server for these tools.

Files

Your images are processed in your browser. We do not upload, sell, inspect, or permanently store your photos when you use the browser tools.

Browser processing

Tools such as compression, resizing, conversion, metadata removal, image to PDF, and passport photo generation use browser APIs on your device.

Accounts

Login may be offered later for optional account features. The current image tools can be used without creating an account.

Analytics and ads

ProtectMyPhoto may add privacy-conscious analytics or advertising later. Any future change should be reflected on this page before production use.

Contact

For privacy questions, email admin@protectmyphoto.in.
