Responsible use notice

This tool is intended for branding, personal, creative, educational, and legitimate business use. Do not use it to imitate government, legal, banking, police, notary, court, identity, licence, certificate, or other regulated official seals.

What is a digital stamp?

A digital stamp is a graphic mark used for branding, status labels, organisation, or creative document workflows.

Can I create a round stamp?

Yes. Choose Round or Oval and use curved top and bottom text controls.

Can I upload my own logo?

Yes. Uploaded logos are processed locally in your browser and are not stored on our servers.

Can I create a transparent stamp?

Yes. Choose Transparent PNG in the export panel.

What file formats can I download?

PNG, JPG, and basic SVG export are supported. PNG is recommended for transparent stamps.

Can I choose a custom stamp size?

Yes. Select Custom Size and enter width, height, and unit.

Can I preview my stamp on a document?

Yes. Use the document preview section to drag, resize, rotate, and adjust opacity on generic sample documents.

Is the stamp maker free?

The browser-side editor works without login and without paid APIs.

Is my uploaded logo stored?

No. In this implementation, logos remain in the browser memory/local draft only on your device.

Can I use the stamp for business branding?

Yes, for legitimate branding, packaging, creative, and business workflows.

Can I create official government seals?

No. Do not use this tool to imitate government, legal, banking, police, notary, court, identity, licence, certificate, or other regulated official seals.
