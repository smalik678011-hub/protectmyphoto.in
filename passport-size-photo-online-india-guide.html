Passport size photo online for India: what to check before upload

Updated 2026-07-12 by ProtectMyPhoto

A practical guide to making passport size photos online for Indian forms with crop, background, and file size tips.

Start with the form requirement

Indian forms often ask for a recent colour photograph, plain background, specific dimensions, and a strict file size such as 20KB, 50KB, or 100KB. The exact rule changes from portal to portal, so check the instruction text before editing.

Crop before you compress

A passport photo should keep the face centered with enough space around the head and shoulders. If you compress before cropping, you may waste file size on unwanted background. Use the Passport Photo Maker first, then compress only if needed.

Background and lighting

A clean white or light background works best for most forms. Avoid heavy shadows, tilted faces, dark filters, and screenshots from social media photos. A simple front-facing phone photo in daylight is usually better than an over-edited image.

Keep one final copy

After download, rename the final file clearly, for example passport-photo-50kb.jpg. Open it once on your phone or computer and make sure the face is sharp before you upload it.

Quick answers

What size is an Indian passport photo?

Many offline passport photos are 35mm x 45mm, but online forms may ask for pixels and KB size instead. Follow the specific portal.

Can I use a selfie?

Use a clear front-facing photo with neutral expression and plain background. Avoid wide-angle distortion and heavy beauty filters.
