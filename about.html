About

India's privacy-first browser image toolkit.

ProtectMyPhoto helps people compress, resize, convert, and prepare images without unnecessary uploads. The tools are designed to be fast, simple, and private on everyday phones and laptops.

Why this exists

Many online forms need strict image dimensions and small file sizes. Many tools upload files before processing. ProtectMyPhoto starts from a simpler promise: process locally whenever the browser can do it safely.

Official domain

The official project domain is protectmyphoto.in. For admin or support requests, email admin@protectmyphoto.in.
