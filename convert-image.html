Format notes

JPG is best for small photo uploads. PNG is best for sharp graphics. WebP is compact and modern, but not every old system accepts it.

Which image format should you choose?

  • Use JPG for most photos and online forms.
  • Use PNG for graphics, screenshots, or transparency.
  • Use WebP for modern web use and smaller files.

Frequently asked questions

Can I convert JPG to PNG online?

Yes. Choose your image, select PNG output, and download the converted file.

Which format is best for forms?

JPG is accepted by most online forms and usually creates smaller photo files.

Is WebP supported everywhere?

WebP is modern and compact, but some older portals may still require JPG or PNG.

Are converted images uploaded?

No. Conversion happens in your browser using local processing.
