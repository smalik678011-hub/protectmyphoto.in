Before submitting

Different passports, visas, exams, and government forms may require different dimensions and file sizes. Always check the official requirement before final submission.

Passport photo tips

  • Use a clear front-facing photo with good lighting.
  • Keep the face centered and leave space above the head.
  • Choose the required size and KB limit from the official form instructions.
  • Use white or off-white background unless the form asks for another color.
